FAQs

How long does implementation take?

Starter goes live in 7–14 days. Growth adds 1–2 weeks for sales flows and reporting. Enterprise varies by data availability.

Is our data secure?

We use OAuth/API keys, least-privilege scopes, encrypted transit/storage where applicable, and you can revoke access anytime. No public model training on your data.

Will the AI match our brand voice?

Yes. We ingest your guidelines and examples, then enforce tone via prompts + guardrails. You can review and tweak in week one.

What results should we expect?

Typical: 45% fewer repetitive tickets, 6–16% conversion lift depending on plan, and 3–12× ROI. Use the ROI calculator above for a tailored estimate.

Do you replace our support team?

No. We deflect repetitive tickets and escalate complex ones to your agents with full context, reducing workload and response times.

Which platforms do you integrate with?

Shopify, Klaviyo/Omnisend, Slack, Google (Sheets/Calendar), Xero/Sage, and more. See the integrations marquee above.

What if results aren’t there?

We offer a 30-day performance guarantee. If agreed outcomes aren’t met, you don’t pay.

Can you support WooCommerce or custom stacks?

Yes. We have adapters for WooCommerce and can build custom connectors for ERPs/CRMs if needed.
